What Do We Do?

At our weekly meetings we discuss current world events, local politics, and everything in between! Meet with us and learn more about the policies affecting your daily life.

We also spend time together outside of the meetings! We have organized protests, formed carpools to give testamony against bills, and much more!

Join us and form friendships that will last well past graduation!

We welcome guests to get their opinion on the world as it is today. Our most recent guest was former First Lady of Ohio, Frances Strickland!
