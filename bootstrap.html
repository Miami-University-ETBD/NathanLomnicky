Who I Am

Hi! My name is Nathan Lomnicky, and I am the person who programmed and styled this website. I am a Games and Simulation/Computer Science major at Miami University. I love to learn and expand my knowledge about everything and anything games and computer related, and am hoping get good enough at the fundementals of web design to be able to make projects for family, freinds, and potentiall even clients!

I love making video games, foxes, my two dogs Ginger and Melody, and sorrounding myself with like-minded people who want to become the most educated they possibly can.

I set out to make this website primarily as a test for a website I am hoping to make for the Miami Democrats on campus. I belive their cause is just and in the best interest for the people of MU and the rest of the world.
